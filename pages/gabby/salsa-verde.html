Salsa Verde

Here's a classic salsa verde recipe- fresh and easy to make. This verison uses tomatillos for that siganture bright green color and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb tomatillos
  • 1 garlic
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1/2 cup of cilantro leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. On a pot add water to boil tomatillos, jalapenos, half white onion, and garlic. Boil for around 10-15 minutes, or at least until the tomatillos are soft and in a darker color.
  2. Drain the water, blend all together and adding some cilantro as well for a better taste.
  3. Once the ingredients are blended is time to pour our salsa into a bowl, mix it together and add salt to taste.
  4. As a suplemental, choppe half white onion and some cilantro. Add to the salsa for more flavor and mix it.
